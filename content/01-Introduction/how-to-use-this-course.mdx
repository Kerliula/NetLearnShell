# Read it like a book. Explore it like a terminal.

The lessons are ordered deliberately — each one builds on the last. If you are
starting from zero, go in order. If you already know some networking, feel free
to jump to whatever is unfamiliar.

## Structure

The course is divided into chapters, each covering one layer of the internet
stack. Within each chapter, lessons get progressively more specific — starting
with the big picture and zooming in.

The sidebar on the left is your map. It shows every chapter and lesson, and
tracks your progress as you move through the material. If you ever feel lost,
open the sidebar — it will tell you exactly where you are and what comes next.

## Going deeper

In the sidebar you can find the **Resources** tab. It collects the best
external material for that topic — RFC documents, video explanations, relevant
chapters from Kurose & Ross, and tools worth knowing about.

The lessons here are built for intuition. The resources are for when you want
to go further. Use them together.
